Wild Me

Wild Me is an NGO dedicated to improving the collection and management of wildlife data. For many researchers, their projects are delayed, potentially even for years, due to lack of technological expertise or budget. Projects that require analysis of a large volume of data, such as images taken by camera traps, may need to be sorted and identified by individuals. This creates a long delay before the results can be compiled, and limits the scope and repeatability of such projects. Wild Me provides open source software that aims to speed up this process using machine learning.

"Wild Me builds open software and artificial intelligence for the conservation research community. We are machine learning experts and software professionals supporting you in the fight against extinction."

Wildbook and Codex

This tool, developed by Wild Me, allows researchers to track individual animals, collect samples and analyse them, involve citizen scientists to collect sighting information, and more. Researchers upload their photos and associated metadata, and the software categorises them. It creates Encounters for each animal identified and a Sighting for each group of multiple animals. These can include data about the relationships between the animals. Individuals have one or more encounters and a profile page containing relevant information that has been confirmed by researchers.


The detection of species and individuals makes use of pretrained machine learning models, including:
  • Pose Invariant Embeddings, which identifies individual animals. It is trained on species by species basis, and significantly, it can register new individuals without needing to be retrained.
  • Hotspotter analyses textures in an image to identify unique patterning, and compares this to images stored in the database.
  • CurvRank v2 and finFindR match an edge, for example, a whale's dorsal fin.
The software allows for joint human and machine learning review of data.

Services

Wild Me also offer services to researchers, including assistance with data upload, configuration and support with Wildbooks and help with annotation of photographic data.
